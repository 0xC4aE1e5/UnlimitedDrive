Crazy cheap storage

Need to take your files on the go? Have limited money? Use UnlimitedDrive. Just buy a very cheap flash drive (in the small gigabytes or big megabytes numbers, don't get into the terabytes, it's a scam), then drag and drop this file, and that's it. It's also recommended to install uBlock Origin into your web browser to avoid getting scammed when downloading your files.

How it works

First, you drag and drop all of your files, and right click Sync.ps1 and run with PowerShell. It will then convert all of your files to links to a file sharing site that has unlimited storage (nobody else will see it, unless they brute-force a link to your file, which is unlikely). To open a file, just open the file and click DOWNLOAD. To reupload the file, just drag and drop it in the same folder as the link and run Sync.ps1 again. It won't reupload the links.
